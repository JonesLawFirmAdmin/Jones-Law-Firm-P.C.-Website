Lawyer Spotlight:

T. Bryce Jones, Esq.

Our Founder

Tanner “Bryce” Jones, Esq., Managing Director of the firm, develops the litigation strategies on all cases and ensures that they match client goals, By listening to and understanding the client's perspective, he is able to give balanced advice on the risk-weighted costs and benefits of undertaking legal work at all stages of the litigation lifecycle. His legal interests include the enforceability of money judgments and the powers and limitations of contracts to create and maintain incentives among businesses and their stakeholders.

Before Jones Law Firm

Before forming his own fim, Beyce was an eDiscovery project manager who deployed cutting edge technology to analyze email and other data in internal investizations of some of the nation’s largest financal institutions. While obtaining his Juris Doctor and Certificate in Intemational Law at Columbia University School of Law, Bryce helped found the Columbia Private Equity Society and was a member of an award-winning Vis Moot Court team.

Bryce is originally from Norman, Oklahoma and currently lives in Manhattan. As a member of the Lawyers’ Assistance Program Committee of the New York City Bar Association, he participates in the rehabilitation of attorneys struggling with mental hetlth or drug and alcohol issues. Bryce dedicates much of his non-professional time to Riseho Kosei-kai, a Buddhist organization emphasizing the teachings of the Lotus Sutra.
